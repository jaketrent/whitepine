I have been a resident of Park City, Utah since I was very young. It is a beautiful place to call home. There's so much to do here and so many good people to associate with. There's always something going on. It's in this wonderful environment that I decided to establish my interior design business.

I have long been interested in interior design -- knowing how to coordinate themes and blend styles, how to set the mood in a room or even an entire home, and being able to express ideas and personality through this art. Because of this interest, I have gone on to receive my degree in Interior Design from Brigham Young University -- Idaho. There I was tutored in much of the technique used in successful interior design. I also received special training and exposure in architecture and design as I studied abroad in France, Italy, and Spain.

After completing my work at the university, I easily transitioned back into this area, first working for a local designer and then starting my own interior design business -- White Pine Design. There has been great response to my services in this area. I have had a steady influx of clientel, most desiring assistance in their development of higher-end residential projects and also a few commercial projects.

At the time of my return to Park City, I married Gregg Simons. Since then, we have both received our Real Estate Licenses are also working together meeting the needs of clients in the ever-growing real estate business in the Park City area. Because of my unique training and experience in interior design, I have been able to work seamlessly with clients that also have interior design needs. It has been a wonderful experience for us.

I never cease to tire of life. There are always new challenges and something exciting to engage in. In our spare time, Gregg and I enjoy adventures in the outdoors, travelling to new places, and visiting our family. We are pleased with our success so far and look forward to a bright future.
